maturāpuri ampikai mālai

kulacēkara pāṇṭiyaṉ



vināyakar kāppu

kaṭṭaḷaik kalittuṟai



naṭakkum tiru viḷaiyāṭṭu ōr aṟupattu nālum cokkar

aṭakkum teṉ kūṭalil ampikai mālaikku aruvi matat,

taṭak kumpa, kampac, ciṟu kaṇ, pukar mukattu, āla vaṭṭam

muṭakkum taṭakkai, oru kōṭṭu vāraṇam muṉ niṟkavē.



nūl



tiruvē! viḷainta centēṉē! vaṭi iṭṭa teḷ amutiṉ

uruvē! maṭap piḷḷai ōtimamē! oṟṟai āṭakap pūn

taruvē! niṉ tāmarait tāḷē caraṇam, caraṇam kaṇṭāy,

aruvē! aṇaṅku aracē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 1.



nāḷ koṇṭa koṅkait tuṇaiyum, poṉ mēṉiyum, nañcu aḷitta

vāḷ koṇṭa nāṭṭamum, toṇṭaic cev vāyum, maruṅku uṭuttut

tōḷ koṇṭa cem paṭṭum, muttu āramum koṇṭu tōṉṟi eṉai

āḷ koṇṭa nāyakiyē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 2.



karumpum, kaṇai aintum, pāca aṅkucamum, kaik koṇṭu aṭiyēṉ

tirumpum ticai toṟum tōṟṟu kaṇṭāy - icai tēkku maṇic

curumpu uṇṭa kāviyum, cōti nilāvum, tuḷirum caṟṟē

arumpum kaṉam kuḻalāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 3.



kuṉṟē eṉum mulaiyār tarum kātal koṭumai ellām

veṉṟēṉ, maṟaliyai viṭṭu viṭṭēṉ, virait tāmarait tāḷ

eṉṟē eṉ ceṉṉi vaittāy, piṉṉai yāṉ ceyyum ēval ellām

aṉṟē uṉ ēval kaṇṭāy, maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 4.



vaṭi vaitta vēl viḻiyār anurāka mayakkil ceṉṟu

kuṭi vaitta neñcu eṉṟu mīḷum kolō! aṉpu koṇṭa toṇṭar

muṭi vaittavāṟu, eṉ pulait talai mēl vaitta muttiṉ taṇṭai

aṭi vaitta pēr iṉpamē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 5.



vaṭak kuṉṟa mēruvum, mūtu aṇṭam eṭṭi vaḷaintu puṟam

kiṭakkum kaṭalum, puvaṉaṅkaḷ ēḻum, kiḷar marupput

taṭak kuñcaram eṭṭum, ellām tiru untit tāmaraiyil

aṭakkum parācaktiyē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 6.



oḷi koṇṭa veṇ piṟait tōṭum, poṉ ōlaiyum, ūṟiya tēṉ

tuḷi koṇṭa ceṅkaṉi vāyum, muttāramum, tōḷum eṉṟaṉ

kaḷi koṇṭa neñcam kuṭi koṇṭavā icai kakku maṇi

aḷi koṇṭa pūṅkuḻalāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 7.



maṇiyum, taraḷamum, veṇ nakaiyō? vaḻi mūvar ceyat

tuṇiyum, toḻilum uṉ cey toḻilō? pattit tutti muṭip

paṇiyum, cuṭarum, kaṭal ēḻum niṉ kaḻal paṅkayattil

aṇiyum tiru uruvē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 8.



ceḻum tuṅkak koṅkaiyum, muttu āramum, poṉ cilampum, tiṅkaḷ

koḻuntum, makarak kuḻaiyum ellām, vaṇṭu koṇṭu cuṟṟi

uḻum tumpai cūṭum tiru mēṉiyum, uṉ uṭalum oṉṟāy

aḻuntum parācaktiyē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 9.


kaṇaiyum, kumiḻum, iṇai neṭum cāpamum, kārum, vaḷḷait

tuṇaiyum, pavaḷamum, cōti nilāvum, tuvaṇṭa paccaip

paṇaiyum, paṉit taṭam kāntaḷum, pāntaḷum pattum oṉṟāy

aṇaiyum tiru uruvē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 10.



iṇaṅkēṉ oruvarai; niṉ iru tāḷ aṉṟi eppoḻutum

vaṇaṅkēṉ oruvarai vāḻttukilēṉ; vañca neñcar uṭaṉ

piṇaṅkēṉ - amutam perukum cem pātip piṟai muṭitta

aṇaṅkē! caraṇam kaṇṭāy, maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 11.



mulaikkē avacat tuyar viḷaippār iṉpa mōkam eṉṉum

valaikkē akappaṭṭu aḻunti viṭāmal, valiya vantu eṉ

talaikkē patam vaitta taṇ aḷiyāy! muttam tattu tirai

alaikkē viḻi tuyilvāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 12.



koṭik koṇṭa ciṟu iṭaiyār maṇi nūpuram koñcum aṭip

poṭik koṇṭa ceṉṉi uṉ kāl vaikkumō? puṟṟu araveṭuttu

muṭik koṇṭa cokkar aḻiyā viratam muṭikka eṉṟē,

aṭik koṇṭa pūṇ mulaiyāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 13.



viḷaiyum karuk kuḻi vīḻāmal, eṉ taṉ viṉaip piṟavi

kaḷaiyum paṭikkum karutu kaṇṭāy - kaḻunīrai veṉṟu

vaḷaiyum taraḷa maṇit tōṭu aḻuttiya vaḷḷaiyaic ceṉṟu

aḷaiyum kayal viḻiyāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 14.



ūṭum poḻutu uṉ karuṇai viṭāmal uvappataṟkuk

kūṭum toḻilai maṟappatu uṇṭō? naṟaik koṉṟai am tār

cūṭum talaivar tiru mārpil, vāri cori taraḷam

āṭum tuṇai mulaiyāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 15.



maḻaik kontu aḷaka maṭavār tam kātal valait talaippaṭṭu

uḻaikkum tuyaram oḻippatu eṉṟō? iraṇṭu ūcal maṇik

kuḻaikkum kalanta pacumai elām kuḻai ūṭu naṭantu

aḻaikkum kayal viḻiyāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 16.



iṇaik kumpa meṉ mulaiyār viḻi vēlukkum, eyyu mataṉ

kaṇaikkum taṉi ilaikkā viṭavō? maṇik kaccu akalāt

tuṇaik kumpa meṉ mulaiyāy! cokkar mēṉi tuvaḷak kaṭṭi

aṇaikkum kamalam uḷḷāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 17.



num kēḷvar pākattum, an nāṉ maṟai eṉum nūl iṭattum,

koṅku ēy pokuṭṭuk kamala ālayattum, kuṭi koṇṭa nī

eṅkē irukkiṉum nāy aṭiyēṉukku iṭar varum pōtu

aṅkē veḷippaṭuvāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 18.



poṉṉē! nava maṇiyē! amutē! puvi pūttu aṭaṅkā

miṉṉē! oḷi uṟṟa mey poruḷē! karu mētiyiṉ mēl

en nēram kālaṉ vantu āvi viṭṭālum, eṉakku añcēl eṉṟu

an nēram vantu aruḷvāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 19.



viruntu uṇṭu pōkaikku nāyēṉ caṭalattai vem kaḻukum,

paruntum cuḻalu muṉṉē varuvāy - maṇap pantalilē

tiruntum karam paṟṟi niṟpār tiru mukac cevvi ellām

aruntum kayal viḻiyāy! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 20.



nāraṇaṉ koñcum pukaḻ nāṉmukaṟkum, poṉ nāṭṭavarkkum

kāraṇam koñcum niṉ ciṟu aṭikkē ticai kaṭṭum eṭṭu

vāraṇam koñcum kaṭampu aṭaviyiṉ makiḻnaruṭaṉ

āraṇam koñcum ammē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 21.



neḷikkum puḻuvukku iṭam ām kurampaiyiṉ niṉṟum, uyir

oḷikkum poḻutu veḷippaṭuvāy - oḻiyāp piṟavic

cuḷikkum kaṭalil cuḻalāmal vāḻvait tuṟantu, paṭaittu,

aḷikkum parācaktiyē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 22.



oḻiyāp paṉit taṭam kaṇ nīr coriya vantu, uṉ aṭikkē

poḻiyāp putu malar iṭṭu niṟpārkku, uṉ poḻi karuṇai

viḻiyāl curappa, alarnta cem tāmarai vīṭu oṉṟavē

aḻiyāp patam taruvāy, maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 23.



ēlam aṭaṅkum kuḻalār kuṟu ēvalil eyttu, aṭiyēṉ

kālam aṭaṅkum muṉṉē varuvāy - viṇ kaṭantu niṉṟa

kōlam aṭaṅkum aṟiyāta paccaik kuḻaviyaip pōl

ālam aṭaṅkum ammē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 24.



pōrkkum kalā matiyum, koṉṟai mālaiyum, poṉ muṭi mēl

cērkkum talaivar muṉ cellum ap pōtu, tiraṇṭa muttiṉ

vārk kuṅkumak koṅkai yāṉaikku muṉṉam maṇi muracam

ārkkum parācaktiyē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 25.



nōkkum karuṇai viḻiyāl potu aṟa nōkki, eṉṉaik

kākkum paṭikkum karutu kaṇṭāy - oḷi kakku nilā

vīkkum caṭai aṭaviyār uṇṭa kāḷa viṭattai amutu

ākkum civa āṉantamē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 26.



ulai kutikkum taḻal ūṟiya kāṉalai uṉṉic ceṉṟu,

kalai kutikkumpaṭi pōl iḻaittēṉ - kaḻic cēl vekuṇṭu

valai kutikkum cem kaḻunīr uṭaintu vaḻinta cem tēṉ

alai kutikkum taṭam cūḻ maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 27.



poṭi paṭṭa piṭṭukkut tōḷ koṇṭu kūṭai maṇ pōṭṭup, poṅki
veṭi paṭṭa vaiyai aṭaitta an nāḷ, cokkar mēṉi cuṟṟum

koṭi paṭṭa pōtu muḻu nīlak kōmaḷa mēṉiyilum

aṭi paṭṭatō? aṇaṅkē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 28.



pūṇṭa kai vārip, putu malar tūvi, niṉ poṉ aṭikkē

kūṇṭa kai, ceṉṉi kuvikkap peṟṟēṉ - piṟaik kōṭu aṇintu,

nīṇṭa kai vēḻap piṭar ēṟi, vaṭṭa nilam purakkum

āṇ takaiyē! aṇaṅkē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 29.



koñcum kutalai miṉṉārkku aṉpu pūṇṭu kulaiyum eṉ taṉ

neñcu, uṉ poṉ pātam niṉaippatu uṇṭō? veṇ nilā matik kūṉ

piñcum, kaṇai villum, aṅkuca pācamum, piñcu malar

añcum tarittavaḷē! maturāpuri ampikaiyē! 30.



nūṟpayaṉ



piṭittāraik kaṭṭi aṇaittu, amutu ūṭṭiya pēyiṉ mulai

kuṭittu āṭum māyaṉ, kulacēkaraṉ, vaṭa kuṉṟaic ceṇṭāl

aṭittāṉ vaṭitta col ampikai mālai, ai āṟu kavi

paṭittārkaḷ kaṟpakak kāvum, poṉ nāṭum paṭaippavarē.
