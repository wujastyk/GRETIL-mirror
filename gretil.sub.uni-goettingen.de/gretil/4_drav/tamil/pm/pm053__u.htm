1. pāṭṭiṉiyalpu



mullaippāṭṭu eṉpataip paṟṟit teriya vēṇṭuvaṉa ellām ārāyummuṉ, pāṭṭu eṉpatu

ettakaiyatu? eṉṟu ārāyntu aṟintu koḷḷal vēṇṭum. piṉṟaik kālattut tamiḻp pulavar
pāṭṭeṉpatu

iṉṉateṉṟē aṟiyārāyp putupputu muṟaiyāṟ coṟkaḷaik kōttup poruḷ āḻamiṉṟic ceyyuḷ

iyaṟṟukiṉṟār. paṇṭaik kālattut taṇṭamiḻp pulavarō pāṭṭu eṉpataṉ iyalpai naṉkaṟintu
nalamuṭaiya

ceyyuṭkaḷ palappala iyaṟṟiṉār. iṅṅaṉam muṟkālattārāṟ ceyyappaṭṭa pāṭṭiṉ iyalpoṭu
māṟupaṭṭup

piṟkālattār uṇmai piṟaḻntu pāṭiya ceyyuṭkaḷaik kaṇṭu māṇākkar pāṭṭiṉiyalpu
aṟiyātu

mayaṅkuvārākaliṟ, pāṭṭu eṉpatu iṉṉateṉpataṉai oru ciṟitu viḷakkuvām.



ulaka iyaṟkaiyiṟ kaṇ mutalāṉa pulaṉkaḷukku viḷaṅkit tōṉṟum aḻakai yellān taṉṉakattē

neruṅkap potintuvaittup, piṉ avaṟṟai nam aṟiviṉiṭattē pulappaṭuvaṇṇan tōṟṟuvittup,
poruḷ

nikaḻcciyoṭu māṟupaṭutal illā iṉiya ōcaiyuṭaṉ icaintu naṭaipeṟum iyalpiṉai
uṭaiyatutāṉ pāṭṭeṉṟu aṟital vēṇṭum. iṉṉum eṅkeṅku nam aṟivait tamvayap
paṭuttukiṉṟa pēraḻakum pēroḷiyum peruntaṉmaiyum

viḷaṅkit tōṉṟukiṉṟaṉavō aṅkellām pāṭṭu uṇṭeṉṟē aṟitalvēṇṭum. itaṉai viḷakkik

kāṭṭumiṭattup, pēraḻakāṟ ciṟanta ōr araci tāṉ mēṟpōrttirunta nīlappaṭṭu āṭaiyiṉaic
ciṟitu

ciṟitāka nīkkip, piṉ ataṉaic curuṭṭik kīḻē eṟintu viṭṭut tuyil oḻintu, oḷiviḷaṅku taṉ

naḷimukaṅkāṭṭi eḻuntatai yoppa, iruṭkūṭṭañ curuṇṭu maṭaṅki alaikaṭaliṟ ceṉṟu
aṭaṅkiviṭumāṟu iḷaiya ñāyiṟu urukkit tiraṭṭiya pacumpoṟ ṟiraḷaipōlat taḷataḷaveṉak
kīḻt ticaiyil tōṉṟavum,

atticaiyiṉ parappellām poṉ uraitta kaṟpōṟ polintu tikaḻavum, pacumai poṉmai nīlam
civappu

veṇmai mutalāṉa niṟa vēṟupāṭuḷḷa poṉ veḷḷikaḷ uruki ōṭukiṉṟa nilam pōla vāṉ
iṭamellām

palavaṇṇamāy virintu viḷaṅkavum, kariyamukilka ḷellāñ cevvarakku vaḻitta akaṉṟa
tiraic cīlaikaḷ

pōlavum āṅkāṅkuc collutaṟ kariya pēroḷiyoṭu tikaḻavum ulakamaṅkai nakaittāṟ pōlap

putumaiyuṟṟut tōṉṟum viṭiyaṟkāla aḻakellām pāṭṭeṉṟē aṟitalvēṇṭum. ā! iṅṅaṉan
tōṉṟum av viṭiyaṟkāla aḻakiṉaik kaṇṭuviyanta vaṇṇamāy mīṉvalaiyoṭu kaṭaṟkaraiyil
niṟkuñ cempaṭavaṉaik kāṭṭiṉuñ ciṟanta pulavaṉ yār?

av viṭiyaṟkālaiyilē mullai nilattu mēypparkaḷ āṇkaṉṟukaḷait toḻuvattilē tāmpiṉāṟ

kaṭṭivaittu āṉniraikaḷai aṭuttuḷḷa malaiccāraliṟ koṇṭupōyp paciya pul mēyaviṭṭut
tām maranaḻaliṟ

cāyntiruntu koṇṭu, tamakketirē paccilaip pōrvai mēṟkoṇṭu kariya mukilkaḷ neṟṟi
taḻuvik

kiṭappap peruntaṉmaiyoṭu vāṉ aḷāvit tōṉṟum malaiyiṉai aṇṇāntu pārttavarāy avarkaḷ
accamum makiḻcciyum aṭaiyum pōtu aṅkum pāṭṭu uṇṭeṉṟē aṟital vēṇṭum.

kātaliṉāṟ kaṭṭuṇṭa iḷaiñarum makaḷirum nekiḻāta kātalaṉpiṉ mikutiyāl tōḷoṭu

tōḷ piṇaiyat taḻuvikkoṇṭu, malaiyaṭivārattil uḷḷa pūñcōlaikaḷiṟ kaḷippāy ulavuntoṟun
tūṅkaṇaṅ kuruvikaḷ marakkiḷaikaḷil viyappāṉa kūṭu kaṭṭutalaiyum; marap
pontukaḷi liruntu maṇippuṟākkaḷ

kūvutalaiyum; āṇmayilkaḷ tam aḻakiya tōkaiyiṉai virittup peṭaimayil kaṇṭukaḷippa
orupuṟam

āṭutalaiyum; malaiyiliruntoḻukum aruvinīr kūḻāṅ kaṟpaṭaiyiṉmēṟ ciluciluveṉṟu
ōṭivantu

accōlaiyiṉ oru pakkattuḷḷa āḻnta kuṭṭattil nirampit tuḷumpa, ataṉkaṇ uḷḷa
centāmarai mukiḻkaḷ

akaṉṟa ilaikaḷiṉmēl itaḻkaḷai virittu mikac civappāy alar talaiyum virumpikkaṇṭu,
naṟumaṇaṅ kamaḻum

pūkkaḷai maraṅkaḷiṉiṉṟun tāvip paṟittuk kariya kūntalil māṟimāṟi aṇintuñ, civakkap
paḻutta

kovvaikkaṉi pōṉṟa tam itaḻkaḷ aḻunta muttam vaittukkoṇṭun, tēṉ oḻukiṉāleṉa iṉiya

nēyamoḻikaḷ pēcikkoṇṭum avarkaḷ cellumiṭattu aṅkum pāṭṭu uṇṭeṉṟē aṟital
vēṇṭum.

curuṅkac colluṅkāl eṅkellām namatuṇarvaik kavarkiṉṟa pēraḻaku ulaka iyaṟkaiyiṟ

kāṇappaṭumō aṅkellām pāṭṭu uṇṭeṉpatu teḷiyappaṭum. āyiṉum, oru nallicaip
pulavaṉāl

iyaṟṟappaṭukiṉṟa pāṭṭuppōla atu nūliṉiṭattē kāṇappaṭuvatillaiyē yeṉiṉ; naṉku
viṉāyiṉāy, oru

nūliṉkaṇ eḻutappaṭṭu, ulaka iyaṟkaiyiṉ aḻakai namatuḷḷattiṟ tōṉṟak kāṭṭi namakku
uvappuṇarvu payakkuñ colliṉ tokutiyāṉa pāṭṭu nūliṉkaṇ eḻutappaṭukiṉṟa
vaṭivuṭaiya parupporuḷākum; ulaka iyaṟkaiyiṉ aḻakōṭu oruṅkottu niṉṟu, kaṇ
mutalāṉa pulaṉvaḻip pukuntu namakku uvap puṇarvu
mikutiyiṉai varuvikkum pāṭṭu vaṭivam illāta nuṇporuḷākum. iṅṅaṉa mākaliṉ ulaka
iyaṟkaiyilellām pāṭṭu uṇṭeṉpatu tuṇipēyā meṉka.

allāmalum, uyir vāḻnāḷil ovvoru nāḷum nammuṭaiya niṉaivukaḷellām uṇavu

tēṭutalilum, poruḷ tokuppatilum, maṉaivimakkaṭku vēṇṭuvaṉa tiraṭṭik
koṭuttalilum, piṟar iṭṭa

ūḻiyañ ceytalilumākap palavāṟu citaṟi arumai perumaiyiṉṟik koṉṉē kaḻintu pōkum
makkaḷuṭaiya

niṉaivukaḷuñ coṟkaḷuñ ceyalkaḷum namakku iṉpan tarāvākaliṉ avaṟṟai aṟiya
vēṇṭumeṉa virumpuvārum ulakil yārum ilar. iṉi, ivvāṟu kaḻiyum nāṭkaḷil orōvorukāl
avar aṟivu vaṟiya niṉaivukaḷiṉ vēṟākap pirintu, ulaka iyaṟkaiyiṟ paṭintu ataṉ
vaṇṇamāyt tirintu
teḷivuṟṟu viḷaṅkumpōtu avvaṟiviṟ curantu perukum ariya periya karuttukkaḷaiyē
nām aṟitaṟku mika viḻaikiṉṟōm. iṅṅaṉan tōṉṟum ariya periya karuttukkaḷiṉ kōvai
oḻuṅkiṉaiyē pāṭṭeṉṟum aṟital vēṇṭum.

iṉṉum, makkaḷ vāḻnāḷ eṉkiṉṟa nīrōṭaiyilē vaṟuniṉaivukaḷāṉa kalaṅkaṟ perunīr

perukic cellumpōtu, ulaka iyaṟkai yeṉṉum malaik kukaikaḷilē arittu eṭuttuvanta
aruṅkaruttukkaḷāṉa poṟṟukaḷ iṭaiyiṭaiyē āḻntu avvōṭaiyiṉ aṭinilattiṟ citarntu
miṉṉikkiṭappa, nallicaip pulavaṉ eṉṉum arippukkāraṉ mika viḻaintu muyaṉṟu
appoṟcitarkaḷai yellām
oṉṟākap poṟukki eṭuttut taṉ matinuṭpa neruppiliṭṭu urukkip pacumpoṟ
piṇṭamākat tiraṭṭit taruvatē pāṭṭu eṉṟum aṟital vēṇṭum.

iṉṉum, makkaḷ aṟivu eṉkiṉṟa tittippāṉa ariya amiḻtam palavakaiyāṉa kuṟṟaṅkaḷoṭuṅ

kalappuṟṟut tūyataṉṟāyp pōka, nallicaippulavaṉ taṉ pēraṟiviṉāl ataṉait teḷiya vaṭittu
ataṉ

iṉcuvaiyiṉai mikutippaṭutti, nāmellām ataṉaip parukip periyatōr āṟutalaṭaiyak
koṭuppāṉ;

aṅṅaṉaṅ koṭukkappaṭun tūya iṉiya aṟiviṉ viḷakkamum pāṭṭeṉṟē aṟital vēṇṭum.
ikkaruttup paṟṟiyē miliṭṭaṉ eṉṉum āṅkilamoḻi valla nallicaip pulavarum,
"pāṭṭeṉpatu makkaḷ maṉa aṟiviṉiṉṟum vaṭittu iṟakkappaṭṭa tūya amiḻtam ām"1
eṉṟu urai kūṟiṉār. itu niṟka.

iṉi, iṅṅaṉam iyaṟṟappaṭukiṉṟa pāṭṭu ulaka iyaṟkaiyaḻakuṭaṉ peritum porunti
naṭattal

vēṇṭum. iṉṉum itaṉai nuṇuki nōkkumiṭattup pāṭṭup pāṭutalil vallavaṉāṉa nallicaip

pulavaṉukkum ulaka iyaṟkaiyiṉaip palavakai vaṇṇaṅkaḷāṟ kuḻaittu varaintu
kāṭṭukiṉṟa ōviyakkāraṉukkum oṟṟumai mika uṇṭeṉpatu teḷḷitiṟ pulappaṭum.
āyiṉum, ōviyakkāraṉ varaikiṉṟa ōviyaṅ kaṭpulaṉukku maṭṭumē tōṉṟuvatākum;
nallicaip pulavaṉ amaikkiṉṟa pāṭṭō kaṇ
mutalāṉa pulaṉkaḷiṉ akattē viḷaṅkum uḷḷattilē ceṉṟu tōṉṟuvatākum. ōviyakkāraṉ tāṉ
eḻuta eṭuttukkoṇṭa poruṭṭōṟṟattaip paṉmuṟaiyum nuṇṇitāka aḷantaḷantu pārttuppiṉ
ataṉait tiṟampaṭa varaintāl maṭṭum aṅṅaṉam varainta ōviyattaik kaṇṭu viyakkiṉṟōm;
tāṉ virittu
viḷakkamāy eḻutavēṇṭum pakutikaḷil avaṉ oru ciṟitu vaḻuviviṭṭāṉāyiṉum
avvōviyattiṉkaṇ namakku viyapput tōṉṟātoḻiyum. nallicaippulavaṉō aṅṅaṉam avaṉaippōl
ovvoṉṟaṉaiyum virivāka viḷakkik kāṭṭa vēṇṭum varuttam uṭaiyāṉ allaṉ. ōviyakkāraṉ
pulaṉ aṟivaip paṟṟi niṟpavaṉ; pulavaṉō maṉa aṟivaip paṟṟi niṟpavaṉ.

pulaṉaṟivō parupporuḷkaḷai virittaṟiyum iyalpiluḷḷatu; maṉavaṟivō appulaṉaṟiviṉ

akattē niṉṟu nuṇṇitām poruḷaiyun tāṉē oru noṭiyil virittaṟiyum āṟṟal vāyntatu!
ammamma!

maṉavaṟiviṉ āṟṟalai yām eṉṉaveṉṟu eṭutturaippēm! aṇuvai oru noṭiyil malaipōṟ
perukac ceyyum, malaiyai maṟunoṭiyil ōr aṇuviṉuṅ kuṟukacceyyum. iṅṅaṉam
viyappāṉa iyalpuṭaiya maṉavaṟiviṉai nallicaip pulavaṉ eṉṉum mantirakāraṉ taṉ
matinuṭpamākiya māttiraik
kōlāl toṭṭa aḷavāṉē atu tiṭukkeṉṟeḻuntu avaṉ virumpiya vaṇṇamellāñ cuḻaṉṟu
cuḻaṉṟāṭum.

iṉṉum itaṉaic ciṟitu viḷakkuvām. ōviyakkāraṉ accuṟuttum akaṉṟatoṟu kariya periya

kāṭṭiṉai eḻutal vēṇṭumāyiṟ palanāḷum palakālum ataṉ iyaṟkaiyiṉai aṟintaṟintu
pārttup, pariya

maraṅkaḷ aṭarntu ōṅki oṉṟōṭoṉṟu piṇaintu veḷiccam pukutāmal taṭai ceytu
niṟṟalaiyum, akkāṭṭiṉ veḷittōṟṟa amaippiṉaiyum, maraṅkaḷiṉ iṭaiyiluḷḷa iṭukku
veḷikaḷil namatu pārvai nuḻaiyuṅkāl avai tōṉṟun taṉmaiyiṉaiyum, uḷḷē iruḷ tirintu
paraviyiruttalaiyum aṅkuḷḷavāṟē ciṟanta pala vaṇṇaṅkaḷaik kuḻaittu iraṭṭut tuṇiyiṉ
mēl mika varunti muyaṉṟu eḻutik kāṭṭal vēṇṭum. iḵtu avaṉukkup perunāḷ viṉaiyāka
muṭiyum. nallicaippulavaṉō, 'pariya maraṅkaḷ aṭarntōṅkip piṇaintu niṟkum iruṇṭa
kāṭu' eṉṟu cila
coṟkaḷait tiṟampaṭac cērttuk kūṟutal oṉṟiṉālēyē oru noṭippoḻutil avvōviyakkāraṉāluṅ

āṟṟaluṭaiyaṉāvaṉ. iḵtu ivaṉukku mika eḷitilē muṭivatoṉṟām. iṅṅaṉam maṉavuṇarviṉai
eḻupputal

ataṉai nuṇuki ārāyntu pāṭṭuppāṭum maṉavuṟuti mikutiyumuṭaiyarāyiruntaṉar.
