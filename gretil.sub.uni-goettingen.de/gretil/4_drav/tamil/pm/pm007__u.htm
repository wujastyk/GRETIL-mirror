2032@

neṟimaiyāl niṉaiya vallār,
katiyiṉaik kañcaṉ māḷak

vaṇaṅkiyeṉ maṉattu vanta,

toṇṭaṉēṉ viṭuki lēṉē (2) 1

2033@
