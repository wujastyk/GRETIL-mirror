maturaikkanjci of mangkuTi marutanar
(work in pattuppaTTu anthologies) (in Tamil Script, TSCii format)
caṅka kāla nūlkaḷāṉa pattup pāṭṭukkaḷil āṟāvatāṉa
maturaik kāñci
-----------------------

pāṭiyavar :: māṅkuṭi marutaṉār
pāṭappaṭṭavaṉ :: talaiyālaṅkāṉattuc ceruveṉṟa pāṇṭiyaṉ
tiṇai :: kāñci
pāvakai :: āciriyappā
motta aṭikaḷ :: 782

-----------------------

ōṅku tirai viyaṉ parappiṉ
oli munnīr varam pākat
tēṉ tūṅkum uyar cimaiya
malai nāṟiya viyaṉ ñālattu
vala mātirattāṉ vaḷi koṭpa
viya ṉāṇmī ṉeṟi yoḻukap
pakaṟ ceyyum ceñ ñāyiṟum
iravuc ceyyum veṇ tiṅkaḷum
mai tīrntu kiḷarntu viḷaṅka
maḻaitoḻil utava mātiraṅ koḻukkat . . .10

toṭuppiṉ āyiram vittiyatu viḷaiya
nilaṉu maraṉum payaṉetirpu nanta
nō yikantu nōkku viḷaṅka
mē taka mikap polinta
ōṅku nilai vayak kaḷiṟu
kaṇṭu taṇṭāk kaṭkiṉ pattu
uṇṭu taṇṭā mikuvaḷat tāṉ
uyar pūrima viḻut teruviṟ
poy yaṟiyā vāymoḻi yāṟ
pukaḻ niṟainta naṉmānta roṭu . . .20

nal lūḻi aṭip paṭarap
pal veḷḷam mīk kūṟa
ulakam āṇṭa uyarntōr maruka
piṇak kōṭṭa kaḷiṟṟuk kuḻumpiṉ
niṇam vāyppeyta pēy makaḷir
iṇai yoliyimiḻ tuṇaṅkaic cīrp
piṇai yūpam eḻun tāṭa
añcu vanta pōrkkaḷat tāṉ
āṇ ṭalai aṇaṅ kaṭuppiṉ
vaya vēntar oṇ kuruti . . . .30

ciṉat tīyiṟ peyarpu poṅkat
teṟa laruṅ kaṭun tuppiṉ
viṟal viḷaṅkiya viḻuc cūrppiṉ
toṭit tōṭkai tuṭup pāka
ā ṭuṟṟa ūṉ cōṟu
neṟi yaṟinta kaṭivā luvaṉ
aṭi yotuṅkip piṟ peyarāp
paṭai yōrkku muru kayara
amar kaṭakkum viyaṉ ṟāṉait
teṉṉavaṟ peyariya tuṉṉarun tuppiṉ . . .40

tolmutu kaṭavuṭ piṉṉar mēya
varaittāḻ aruvip poruppiṟ poruna
viḻuc cūḻiya viḷaṅkō ṭaiya
kaṭuñ ciṉatta kamaḻkaṭā attu
aḷaṟu paṭṭa naṟuñ ceṉṉiya
varai maruḷum uyar tōṉṟala
viṉai naviṉṟa pēr yāṉai
ciṉañ ciṟantu kaḷaṉu ḻakkavum
mā veṭutta malikurūut tukaḷ
akal vāṉattu veyil karappavum . . .50

vām pariya kaṭuntiṇ ṭēr
kāṟ ṟeṉṉak kaṭitu koṭpavum
vāḷ miku maṟa maintar
tōḷ muṟaiyāṉ vīṟu muṟṟavum
iruperu vēntaroṭu vēḷir cāyap
poru tavaraic ceru veṉṟum
ilaṅ karuviya varai nīntic
curam pōḻnta ika lāṟṟal
uyarn tōṅkiya viḻuc ciṟappiṉ
nilan tanta pē rutavip . . . .60

polantār mārpi ṉeṭiyōṉ umpal
maran tiṉṉūu varai yutirkkum
narai yurumiṉ ēṟa ṉaiyai
aruṅ kuḻumiḷaik kuṇṭuk kiṭaṅkiṉ
uyarn tōṅkiya niraip putaviṉ
neṭu matil nirai ñāyil
am pumiḻ ayi laruppan
taṇ ṭātu talaic ceṉṟu
koṇṭu nīṅkiya viḻuc ciṟappiṉ
teṉ kumari vaṭa peruṅkal . . . .70

kuṇa kuṭa kaṭalā vellait
toṉṟu moḻintu toḻil kēṭpa
veṟṟa moṭu veṟut toḻukiya
koṟṟa vartaṅ kōṉā kuvai
vāṉi yainta iru munnīrp
pēem nilaiiya irum peḷavattuk
koṭum puṇari vilaṅku pōḻak
kaṭuṅ kāloṭu karai cēra
neṭuṅ koṭimicai itai yeṭuttu
iṉ ṉicaiya muraca muḻaṅkap . . . .80

poṉ malinta viḻup paṇṭam
nā ṭāra naṉ kiḻitarum
āṭi yaṟ peru nāvāy
maḻai muṟṟiya malai puraiyat
tuṟai muṟṟiya tuḷaṅ kirukkait
teṇ kaṭaṟ kuṇ ṭakaḻic
cīr cāṉṟa uyar nelliṉ
ūr koṇṭa uyar koṟṟava
nīrt tevvum nirait toḻuvar
pāṭu cilampu micai yēṟṟat . . . .90

tōṭu vaḻaṅkum aka lāmpiyiṟ
kaya ṉakaiya vaya ṉiṟaikku
meṉ ṟoṭai vaṉ kiḻāar
atari koḷpavar pakaṭupūṇ teṇmaṇi
irumpuḷ oppum icaiyē eṉṟum
maṇippū muṇṭakattu maṇalmali kāṉaṟ
paratavar makaḷir kuravaiyo ṭolippa
orucār, viḻavuniṉṟa viya lāṅkaṇ
muḻavut tōḷ muraṭ porunarkku
uru keḻu peruñ ciṟappiṉ . . . .100

iru peyarp pērā yamoṭu
ilaṅku maruppiṟ kaḷiṟu koṭuttum
polan tāmaraip pūc cūṭṭiyum
nalañ cāṉṟa kalañ citaṟum
pal kuṭṭuvar vel kōvē!
kal kāyuṅ kaṭuvēṉi loṭu
iru vāṉam peyalo ḷippiṉum
varum vaikal mīṉ piṟaḻiṉum
veḷḷamā ṟātu viḷaiyuḷ peruka
nelli ṉōtai arinar kampalai . . .110

puḷḷimiḻn tolikkum icaiyē eṉṟum
calam pukaṉṟu kaṟavuk kalitta
pulavu nīr viyaṉ peḷavattu
nilavuk kāṉal muḻavut tāḻaik
kuḷirp potumpar naḷit tūval
niraitimil vēṭṭuvar karaicēr kampalai
iruṅkaḻic ceṟuviṉ veḷḷuppup pakarnaroṭu
oli yōvāk kali yāṇar
mutu veḷḷilai mīk kūṟum
viyaṉ mēval viḻuc celvattu . . . .120

iru vakaiyāṉ icai cāṉṟa
ciṟu kuṭip perun toḻuvar
kuṭi keḻīiya nāṉilava roṭu
toṉṟu moḻintu toḻil kēṭpak
kā leṉṉak kaṭitu rāay
nāṭu keṭa eri parappi
ālaṅ kāṉat tañcuvara viṟuttu
aracu paṭa amaru ḻakki
muracu koṇṭu kaḷam vēṭṭa
aṭu tiṟaluyar pukaḻ vēntē . . . .130

naṭṭavar kuṭi yuyark kuvai
ceṟṟavar aracu peyark kuvai
pēru lakattu mēen tōṉṟic
cīru ṭaiya viḻuc ciṟappiṉ
viḷaintu mutirnta viḻu muttiṉ
ilaṅku vaḷai iruñ cērik
kaṭ koṇṭik kuṭip pākkattu
naṟ koṟkai yōrnacaip poruna
ceṟṟa tevvar kalaṅkat talaicceṉṟu
añcuvarat taṭkum aṇaṅkuṭait tuppiṟ . . .140

kōḻū uṉkuṟaik koḻu valcip
pulavu viṟ poli kūvai
oṉṟu moḻi oli yiruppil
teṉ paratavar pō rēṟē
ariya vellām eḷitiṉiṟ koṇṭu
uriya vellām ōmpātu vīci
naṉipukaṉ ṟuṟaitu meṉṉā tēṟṟeḻuntu
paṉivār cimaiyak kāṉam pōki
akanāṭu pukkavar viruppam veḷavi
yāṇṭupala kaḻiya vēṇṭupulat tiṟuttu . . .150

mēmpaṭa marīiya velpōrk kurucil
uṟu ceṟunar pulam pukkavar
kaṭi kāvi ṉilai tolaicci
iḻi paṟiyāp peruntaṇ paṇai
kurūuk koṭiya eri mēya
nā ṭeṉum pēr kāṭāka
ā cēnta vaḻi mācēppa
ūri runta vaḻi pāḻāka
ilaṅku vaḷai maṭa maṅkaiyar
tuṇaṅkai yañcīrt taḻūu maṟappa . . .160

avai yirunta perum potiyiṟ
kavai yaṭik kaṭu nōkkattup
pēy makaḷir peyar pāṭa
aṇaṅku vaḻaṅku makalāṅ kaṇ
nilat tāṟṟuṅ kuḻūup putaviṉ
arantaip peṇṭir iṉaintaṉar akavak
koḻum patiya kuṭi tēmpac
ceḻuṅ kēḷir niḻal cēra
neṭunakar vīḻnta karikutirp paḷḷik
kuṭumik kūkai kurāloṭu muralak . . .170

kaḻunīr polinta kaṇṇakaṉ poykaik
kaḷiṟumāy ceruntiyoṭu kaṇpamaṉ ṟūrtara
nallēr naṭanta nacaicāl viḷaivayal
paṉmayirp piṇavoṭu kēḻal ukaḷa
vāḻā maiyiṉ vaḻitavak keṭṭup
pāḻā yiṉaniṉ pakaivar tēem
eḻāat tōḷ imiḻmu ḻakkiṉ
māat tāḷ uyar maruppiṟ
kaṭuñ ciṉatta kaḷiṟu parappi
viri kaṭal viyaṉ ṟāṉaiyoṭu . . .180

muru kuṟaḻap pakaittalaic ceṉṟu
akal vicumpiṉ ārp pimiḻap
peya luṟaḻak kaṇai citaṟip
pala puravi nī ṟukaippa
vaḷai narala vayi rārppap
pī ṭaḻiyak kaṭan taṭṭavar
nā ṭaḻiyak eyil veḷavic
cuṟṟa moṭu tū vaṟuttaliṟ
ceṟṟa tevvar niṉvaḻi naṭappa
viyaṉkaṇ mutupoḻil maṇṭila muṟṟi . . .190

araciyal piḻaiyā taṟaneṟi kāṭṭip
periyōr ceṉṟa aṭivaḻip piḻaiyātu
kuṭamutal tōṉṟiya toṉṟutoḻu piṟaiyiṉ
vaḻivaḻic ciṟakkaniṉ valampaṭu koṟṟam
kuṇamutal tōṉṟiya āriruḷ matiyiṉ
tēyvaṉa keṭukaniṉ tevvar ākkam
uyarnilai ulakam amiḻtoṭu peṟiṉum
poycēṇ nīṅkiya vāynaṭ piṉaiyē
muḻaṅkukaṭa lēṇi malartalai yulakamoṭu
uyarnta tēettu viḻumiyōr variṉum . . .200

pakaivark kañcip paṇintoḻu kalaiyē
teṉpula maruṅkiṉ viṇṭu niṟaiya
vāṇaṉ vaitta viḻuniti peṟiṉum
paḻinamak keḻuka eṉṉāy viḻuniti
ītal uḷḷamoṭu icaivēṭ kuvaiyē
aṉṉāy niṉṉoṭu muṉṉilai evaṉō
koṉṉoṉṟu kiḷakkuval aṭupōr aṇṇal
kēṭṭiciṉ vāḻi keṭukaniṉ avalam
keṭātu nilaiiyarniṉ cēṇviḷaṅku nallicai
tavāp perukkat taṟā yāṇar . . . .210

aḻit tāṉāk koḻun tiṟṟi
iḻit tāṉāp pala coṉṟi
uṇ ṭāṉāk kūr naṟavil
tiṉ ṟāṉā iṉa vaika
ṉilaṉeṭuk kallā voṇpal veṟukkaip
payaṉaṟa vaṟiyā vaḷaṅkeḻu tirunakar
narampiṉ muralu nayamvaru muraṟci
viṟaliyar vaṟuṅkaik kuṟuntoṭi ceṟippap
pāṇar uvappa kaḷiṟupala tarīik
kalantō ruvappa veyiṟpala kaṭaii . . .220

maṟaṅ kalaṅkat talaic ceṉṟu
vāḷuḻan tataṉ tāḷ vāḻtti
nā ḷīṇṭiya nal lakavarkkut
tē rōṭu mā citaṟic
cū ṭuṟṟa cuṭarp pūviṉ
pāṭu pularnta naṟuñ cāntiṉ
viḻumiya periyōr cuṟṟa mākak
kaḷḷiṉ irumpaik kalañcela vuṇṭu
paṇintōr tēen tamvaḻi naṭappap
paṇiyār tēem paṇittuttiṟai koṇmār . .230

paruntupaṟak kallāp pārvaṟ pācaṟaip
paṭukaṇ muracaṅ kālai yiyampa
veṭipaṭak kaṭantu vēṇṭupulat tiṟutta
paṇaikeḻu peruntiṟaṟ palvēl maṉṉar
karaiporu tiraṅkum cuṉaiyiru munnīrt
tiraiyiṭu maṇaliṉum palarē uraicela
malartalai yulakam āṇṭukaḻin tōrē
ataṉāl, kuṇakaṭal koṇṭu kuṭakaṭalmuṟṟi
iravu mellaiyum viḷiviṭa ṉaṟiyātu
avalu micaiyu nīrttiraḷ pīṇṭik . . .240

kavalaiyaṅ kuḻumpiṉ aruvi olippak
kaḻaivaḷar cāraṟ kaḷiṟṟiṉa naṭuṅka
varaimuta liraṅkum ēṟoṭu vāṉñemirntu
citaraṟ perumpeyal ciṟattaliṟ ṟāṅkātu
kuṇakaṭaṟ kivartaruṅ kurūuppuṉa lunti
nivantuce ṉīttaṅ kuḷaṅkoḷac cāṟṟik
kaḷiṟu māykkuṅ katirk kaḻaṉi
oḷi ṟilañci aṭai nivanta
muṭ ṭāḷa cuṭart tāmarai
kaṭ kamaḻu naṟu neytal . . . .250

vaḷ ḷitaḻ aviḻ nīlam
mel lilai yari yāmpaloṭu
vaṇṭiṟai koṇṭa kamaḻpūm poykaik
kampuṭ cēval iṉṟuyil iriya
vaḷḷai nīkki vayamīṉ mukantu
koḷḷai cāṟṟiya koṭumuṭi valaiñar
vēḻap paḻaṉattu nūḻi lāṭṭuk
karumpi ṉentiraṅ kaṭpi ṉōtai
aḷḷaṟ ṟaṅkiya pakaṭuṟu viḻumaṅ
kaḷḷār kaḷamar peyarkkum ārppē . . .260

olinta pakaṉṟai viḷainta kaḻaṉi
vaṉkai viṉaiñar aripaṟai yiṉkural
taḷimaḻai poḻiyun taṇparaṅ kuṉṟiṟ
kavikoḷ cummai yoliko ḷāyan
tatainta kōtai tāroṭu poliyap
puṇarntuṭa ṉāṭum icaiyē yaṉaittum
akaliru vāṉat timiḻntiṉi ticaippak
kuruku narala maṉai marattāṉ
mīṉ cīvum pāṇ cēriyoṭu
marutañ cāṉṟa taṇpaṇai cuṟṟiorucārc . .270

ciṟutiṉai koyyak kavvai kaṟuppak
karuṅkāl varakiṉ iruṅkural pulara
āḻnta kuḻumpil tirumaṇi kiḷara
eḻunta kaṭaṟṟi ṉaṉpoṉ koḻippap
peruṅkaviṉ peṟṟa ciṟutalai neḷavi
maṭakkaṭ piṇaiyoṭu maṟukuvaṉa ukaḷac
cuṭarppūṅ koṉṟai tāaya nīḻaṟ
pāa yaṉṉa pāṟai yaṇintu
nīlat taṉṉa paimpayir micaitoṟum
veḷḷi yaṉṉa voḷvi yutirntu . . .280

curimukiḻ mucuṇṭaiyoṭu mullai tāay
maṇimaru ṇeytal uṟaḻak kāmar
tuṇinīr mellavaṟ ṟoyyiloṭu malara
valloṉ taiiya veṟikkaḷaṅ kaṭuppa
mullai cāṉṟa puṟavaṇin torucār
naṟuṅkāḻ koṉṟu kōṭṭiṉ vittiya
kuṟuṅkatirt tōrai neṭuṅkāl aiyavi
aivaṉa veṇṇelo ṭarilkoḷpu nīṭi
iñci mañcaṭ paiṅkaṟi piṟavum
palvēṟu tāramoṭu kallakat tīṇṭit . . .290

tiṉaiviḷai cāraṟ kiḷikaṭi pūcal
maṇippū avaraik kurūuttaḷir mēyum
āmā kaṭiyuṅ kāṉavar pūcal
cēṇōṉ akaḻnta maṭivāyp payampiṉ
vīḻmukak kēḻal aṭṭa pūcal
karuṅkāl vēṅkai iruñciṉaip poṅkar
naṟumpūk koyyum pūcal iruṅkēḻ
ēṟaṭu vayappulip pūcalo ṭaṉaittum
ilaṅkuveḷ ḷaruviyoṭu cilampakat tiraṭṭak
karuṅkāṟ kuṟiñci cāṉṟa veṟpaṇintu . . .300

aruṅkaṭi māmalai taḻīi orucār
iruvetirp paintūṟu kūreri naippa
niḻatta yāṉai mēypulam paṭarak
kalitta iyavar iyantoṭ ṭaṉṉa
kaṇviṭu puṭaiyūut taṭṭai kaviṉaḻintu
aruvi yāṉṟa aṇiyil māmalai
vaikaṇ ṭaṉṉa puṉmuḷi yaṅkāṭṭuk
kamaḻcūḻ kōṭai viṭaraka mukantu
kāluṟu kaṭaliṉ olikkuñ cummai
ilaivēy kurampai uḻaiyataṭ paḷḷi . . .310

uvalaik kaṇṇi vaṉcol iḷaiñar
cilaiyuṭaik kaiyar kavalai kāppa
niḻaluru viḻanta vēṉiṟkuṉ ṟattup
pālai cāṉṟa curañcērn torucār
muḻaṅkukaṭal tanta viḷaṅkukatir muttam
arampōḻn taṟutta kaṇṇēr ilaṅkuvaḷai
paratar tanta palvēṟu kūlam
iruṅkaḻic ceṟuviṟ ṟīmpuḷi veḷḷuppup
parantōṅku varaippiṉ vaṉkait timilar
koḻumīṉ kuṟaiiya tuṭikkaṇ tuṇiyal . . .320

viḻumiya nāvāy perunī rōccunar
naṉantalai tēettu naṉkalaṉ uymmār

nīṭukoṭi yilaiyiṉar kōṭucuṭu nūṟṟiṉar

celcuṭarp pacuveyiṟ ṟōṉṟi yaṉṉa

ciṟanta karumai nuṇviṉai nuṇaṅkaṟal
niṟaṅkavarpu puṉainta nīlak kacciṉar
meṉṉūl ēṇip paṉmāṇ cuṟṟiṉar . . .640

nilaṉakaḻ uḷiyar kalaṉacaiik koṭkum
kaṇmā ṟāṭavar oṭukka moṟṟi
vayakkaḷiṟu pārkkum vayappuli pōlat
tuñcāk kaṇṇar añcāk koḷkaiyar
aṟintōr pukaḻnta āṇmaiyar ceṟinta
nūlvaḻip piḻaiyā nuṇaṅkunuṇ ṭērcci
ūrkāp pāḷar ūkkaruṅ kaṇaiyiṉar
tērvaḻaṅku teruvi ṉīrtiraṇ ṭoḻuka
maḻaiyamain tuṟṟa arainā ḷamayamum
acaivilar eḻuntu nayamvantu vaḻaṅkaliṟ . . .650

kaṭavuḷ vaḻaṅkuṅ kaiyāṟu kaṅkulum
acca maṟiyā tēma mākiya
maṟṟai yāmam pakaluṟak kaḻippip
pōtupiṇi viṭṭa kamaḻnaṟum poykait
tātuṇ tumpi pōtu muraṉṟāṅ
kōta lantaṇar vētam pāṭac
cīriṉitu koṇṭu narampiṉi tiyakki
yāḻōr marutam paṇṇak kāḻōr
kaṭuṅkaḷiṟu kavaḷaṅ kaippa neṭuntērp
paṇainilaip puravi pulluṇāt teviṭṭap . . .660

palvēṟu paṇṇiyak kaṭaimeḻuk kuṟuppak
kaḷḷōr kaḷitoṭai nuvala illōr
nayanta kātalar kavavuppiṇit tuñcip
pularntuviri viṭiya leyta virumpik
kaṇporā veṟikku miṉṉukkoṭi puraiya
oṇpo ṉaviriḻai teḻippa iyalit
tiṇcuvar nalliṟ katavaṅ karaiya
uṇṭumakiḻ taṭṭa maḻalai nāviṟ
paḻañceruk kāḷar taḻaṅkukural tōṉṟac
cūtar vāḻnta mākatar nuvala . . . .670

vētā ḷikaroṭu nāḻikai icaippa
imiḻmura ciraṅka ēṟumāṟu cilaippap
poṟimayir vāraṇam vaikaṟai iyampa
yāṉaiyaṅ kurukiṉ cēvaloṭu kāmar
aṉṉaṅ karaiya aṇimayil akavap
piṭipuṇar peruṅkaḷiṟu muḻaṅka muḻuvalik
kūṭṭuṟai vayamāp puliyoṭu kuḻuma
vāṉa nīṅkiya nīṉiṟa vicumpiṉ
miṉṉunimirn taṉaiya rāki naṟavumakiḻntu
māṇiḻai makaḷir pulantaṉar parinta . . .680

parūukkā ḻārañ corinta muttamoṭu
poṉcuṭu neruppi ṉilamuk keṉṉa
ammeṉ kurumpaik kāypaṭupu piṟavun
tarumaṇaṉ muṟṟat tariñimi ṟārppa
meṉpūñ cemmaloṭu naṉkalañ cīppa
iravuttalaip peyaru mēma vaikaṟai
maipaṭu peruntōḷ maḻava rōṭṭi
iṭaippulat toḻinta ēntukōṭ ṭiyāṉai
pakaippulaṅ kavarnta pāyparip puravi
vēlkō lāka āḷcela nūṟik . . .690

kāyciṉa muṉpiṟ kaṭuṅkaṭ kūḷiyar
ūrcuṭu viḷakkiṟ ṟanta āyamum
nāṭuṭai nalleyil aṇaṅkuṭait tōṭṭi
nāṭoṟum viḷaṅkak kaitoḻūup paḻicci
nāṭara vanta viḻukkala maṉaittum
kaṅkaiyam pēriyāṟu kaṭaṟpaṭarn tāṅku
aḷantukaṭai yaṟiyā vaḷaṅkeḻu tāramoṭu
puttē ḷulakam kaviṉik kāṇvara
mikkuppukaḻ eytiya perumpeyar maturaic
ciṉaitalai maṇanta curumpupaṭu centī . . .700

oṇpūm piṇṭi aviḻnta kāviṟ
cuṭarpoḻin tēṟiya viḷaṅkukatir ñāyiṟṟu
ilaṅkukati riḷaveyiṟ ṟōṉṟi yaṉṉa
tamaṉiyam vaḷaiiya tāvil viḷaṅkiḻai
nilamviḷak kuṟuppa mētakap polintu
mayilō raṉṉa cāyal māviṉ
taḷirē raṉṉa mēṉit taḷirppuṟattu
īrkki ṉarumpiya titalaiyar kūreyiṟ
ṟoṇkuḻai puṇariya vaṇṭāḻ kātiṟ
kaṭavuṭ kayattamaṉṟa cuṭaritaḻt tāmarait . . .710

tātupaṭu perumpōtu puraiyum vāṇmukat
tāytoṭi makaḷir naṟuntōḷ puṇarntu
kōtaiyiṟ polinta cēkkait tuñcit
tiruntutuyil eṭuppa iṉiti ṉeḻuntu
tiṇkā ḻāra nīvik katirviṭu
moṇkāḻ āraṅ kavaiiya mārpiṉ
varikkaṭaip piraca mūcuvaṉa moyppa
eruttan tāḻnta viravuppūn teriyaṟ
polañceyap polinta nalampeṟu viḷakkam
valikeḻu taṭakkait toṭiyoṭu cuṭarvarac . .720

cōṟamai vuṟṟa nīruṭaik kaliṅkam
uṭaiyaṇi poliyak kuṟaiviṉṟu kavaii
vallōṉ taiiya varippuṉai pāvai
murukiyaṉ ṟaṉṉa uruviṉai yāki
varupuṉaṟ kaṟciṟai kaṭuppa viṭaiyaṟuttu
oṉṉā rōṭṭiya ceruppukal maṟavar
vāḷvaḷam puṇarntaniṉ tāḷvalam vāḻtta
villaik kavaiik kaṇaitāṅku mārpiṉ
mātāṅ keṟuḻttōḷ maṟavart tammiṉ
kalliṭit tiyaṟṟiya iṭṭuvāyk kiṭaṅkiṉ . . .730

nalleyi luḻanta celvart tammiṉ
kollēṟṟup paintōl cīvātu pōrtta
mākkaṇ muracam ōvila kaṟaṅka
erinimirn taṉṉa tāṉai nāppaṇ
perunal yāṉai pōrkkaḷat toḻiya
viḻumiya vīḻnta kuricilart tammiṉ
puraiyōrkkut toṭutta polampūn tumpai
nīryār eṉṉātu muṟaikarutupu cūṭṭik
kāḻmaṇ ṭeḵkamoṭu kaṇaiyalaik kalaṅkip
piripiṇai yarinta niṟañcitai kavayattu . . .740

vāṉat taṉṉa vaḷanakar poṟpa
nōṉkuṟaṭ ṭaṉṉa ūṉcāy mārpiṉ
uyarnta utavi ūkkalart tammiṉ
nivanta yāṉaik kaṇanirai kavarnta
pularnta cāntiṉ viravuppūn teriyaṟ
peruṟcey āṭavart tammiṉ piṟarum
yāvarum varuka ēṉōrun tammeṉa
varaiyā vāyiṟ ceṟāa tiruntu
pāṇar varuka pāṭṭiyar varuka
yāṇarp pulavaroṭu vayiriyar varukeṉa . . .750

iruṅkiḷai purakkum iravalark kellām
koṭuñci neṭuntēr kaḷiṟṟoṭum vīcik
kaḷan tōṟum kaḷ ḷarippa
maran tōṟu mai vīḻppa
niṇa vūṉcuṭ ṭuruk kamaiya
ney kaṉintu vaṟai yārppak
kurūuk kuyppukai maḻai maṅkuliṟ
parantu tōṉṟā viya ṉakarāṟ
pal cālai mutu kuṭumiyiṉ
nal vēḷvit tuṟai pōkiya . . . .760

tol lāṇai nal lāciriyar
puṇarkūṭ ṭuṇṭa pukaḻcāl ciṟappiṉ
nilantaru tiruvi ṉeṭiyōṉ pōla
viyappuñ cālpuñ cemmai cāṉṟōr
palarvāyp pukaraṟu ciṟappiṟ ṟōṉṟi
ariya tantu kuṭi yakaṟṟip
periya kaṟ ṟicai viḷakki
munnīr nāppaṇ ñāyiṟu pōlavum
paṉmīṉ naṭuvaṇ tiṅkaḷ pōlavum
pūtta cuṟṟamoṭu polintiṉitu viḷaṅkip . . .770
poyyā nallicai niṟutta puṉaitārp
perumpeyar māṟaṉ talaiva ṉākak
kaṭantaṭu vāyvāḷ iḷampal kōcar
iyaṉeṟi marapiṉiṉ vāymoḻi kēṭpap
polampūṇ aivar uṭpaṭap pukaḻnta
maṟamiku ciṟappiṟ kuṟunila maṉṉa
ravarum piṟakum tuvaṉṟip
poṟpuviḷaṅku pukaḻavai niṟpukaḻn tētta
ilaṅkiḻai makaḷir polaṅkalat tēntiya
maṇaṅkamaḻ tēṟal maṭuppa nāḷum . . .780

makiḻntiṉi tuṟaimati perum
varaintunī peṟṟa nallū ḻiyaiyē.
