kumarakuruparar aruḷiya

maturai mīṉāṭciyammai piḷḷaittamiḻ



kāppu

vināyaka vaṇakkam



kārkoṇṭa kavuṇmatak kaṭaiveḷḷa muṅkaṭ

kaṭaikkaṭaik kaṉalu mellai

kaṭavātu taṭavuk kuḻaiccevi mukanteṟi

kaṭaikkā ṟiṭṭa veṅkōṉ



pōrkoṇṭa veṇṭōṭpolaṉkuvaṭu potiyumveṇ

poṭituṭi yaṭittu vaittup

puḻutiyāṭ ṭayarāvo rayirāva ṇattulavu

poṟkaḷiṟ ṟaittu tippān



tārkoṇṭa matimuṭi yoruttaṉ ṟirukkaṇmalar

cāttak kiḷarntu poṅkit

tavaḻumiḷa veyilumaḻa nilavumaḷa vaḷavalāṟ

ṟaṇṇeṉṟu vecceṉṟu poṉ



vārkoṇṭa ṭaṇintamulai malaivalli karppūra

valliyapi rāma valli

māṇikka vallimara katavalli yapiṭēka

valli coṟ ṟamiḻ taḻaiyavē.







1-vatu kāppup paruvam







tirumāl



maṇikoṇṭa neṭunēmi valayañ cumantāṟṟu

mākaṇac cūṭṭu mōṭṭu

mālkaḷiṟu piṭarvaitta vaḷaroḷi vimāṉattu

vāluḷai maṭaṅka ṟāṅkum



aṇikoṇṭa pīṭikaiyi ṉampoṉmuṭi muṭivaittē

maiyaṉoṭu vīṟṟi rukku

maṅkayaṟ kaṇṇamutai maṅkaiyark karaciyaiye

mammaṉaiyai yiṉitu kākka



kaṇikoṇṭa taṇṭuḻāyk kāṭalait tōṭutēṅ

kaḻaluḻipāyn taḷaṟu ceyyak

kaḻaṉipaṭu naṭavaiyiṟ kamalat taṇaṅkaracor

kaiyaṇai mukantu cellap



paṇikoṇṭa tuttip paṭappāyc curuṭṭup

paṇaittō ḷerutta laippap

paḻamaṟaikaṇ muṟaiyiṭap paintamiḻp piṉceṉṟa

paccaip pacuṅ koṇṭalē. (1)



paramacivaṉ

vēṟu



cikara vaṭavarai kuṉiya nimirtaru

ceruvi loruporu villeṉakkōṭṭiṉar

ceṭikoḷ paṟitalai yamaṇa retiretir

celavor matalaicol vaiyaiyiṟ kūṭṭiṉar


tiruvu mimaiyavar taruvu maravoli

ceyava lavarkoḷa nalkukait tīṭṭiṉar

ciṟiya veṉa tupuṉ moḻiyum vaṭitamiḻ

teriyu mavarmutu colleṉac cūṭṭiṉar



pakaru micaiticai parava viruvarkaḷ

payilu miyaṟeri veḷvaḷait tōṭṭiṉar

paciya vaṟukoṭu veḷiya nilaviri

pavaḷa vaṉamaṭar palcaṭaik kāṭṭiṉar



patuma mutalvaṉu meḻuta variyator

paṉuva leḻutiya vaitikap pāṭṭiṉar

paracu miracata capaiyi ṉaṭamiṭu

parata patayuka muḷḷamvait tēttun



takaramoḻukiya kuḻalu nilavumiḻ

taraḷa nakaiyume maiyaṉaip pārttetir

caruvi yamarporu viḻiyu maṟukiṭai

taḷara vaḷarvator cevvimuṟ ṟākkaṉa



taṉamu maṉaṉuṟa veḻuti yeḻutaru

tamatu vaṭivaiyu meḷḷimaṭ ṭūṟṟiya

tavaḷa malarvaru miḷami ṉoṭucata

taḷamiṉ vaḻipaṭu taiyalait tūttirai



makara meṟikaṭa lamutai yamutuku

maḻalai paḻakiya kiḷḷaiyaip pēṭṭaṉa

maṭava naṭaipayil piṭiyai viraiceṟi

varaicey puyamicai vaiyam vaittāṟṟiya



vaḻutiyuṭaiya kaṇ maṇiyo ṭulavu peṇ

maṇiyai yaṇitikaḻ celviyait tēkkamaḻ

matura moḻukiya tamiḻi ṉiyalpayiṉ

maturai marakata valliyaik kākkavē. (2)



citti viṉāyakar

vēṟu



kaittala mōṭiru karaṭak karaittirai

kaikkuka ṭāmuṭaik kaṭaliṟ kuḷittemar

cittama tāmoru taṟiyiṟ ṟuvakkuṟu

cittivi nāyaka ṉicaiyaip paḻiccutum



puttamu tōvaru ṭaḻaiyat taḻaittator

poṟkōṭi yōveṉa maturit tuvaṭṭeḻu

muttamiḻ tērtaru maturait talattuṟai

muttaṉa mēvupe ṇaracaip purakkavē. (3)



murukavēḷ

vēṟu



pakara maṭuppak kaṭāmeṭut tūṟṟumor

pakaṭu naṭattip pulōmacaic cūṟpuyal

paruki yiṭakkaṟ pakāṭavip pāṟpoli

paravai yiṭaip paṟ pamāteṉat tōṟṟiya



kumari yirukkak kalāmayiṟ kūttayar

kuḷirpuṉa moyttiṭ ṭacāraliṟ pōycciṟu

kuṟavar makaṭkuc calāmiṭaṟ kēkkaṟu

kumaraṉai muttukkumāraṉaip pōṟṟutum



imiḻtirai muṟṟat tumērumat tārttumuḷ

ḷeyiṟu naccup paṇāṭavit tāppicait

tiṟuka viṟukkit tuḻāy muṭit tīrttaṉo

ṭevaru matittup parāpavat tīccuṭa



vamutucey vittiṭ ṭapōṉakat tāṟcuṭa
raṭaru miruṭṭuk kirīvamaṭ ṭākkiya

vaḻakiya cokkaṟ kumālceyat tōṭṭika

lamarcey kayaṟkaṭ kumāriyaik kākkavē. (4)



nāṉmukaṉ

vēṟu



mēkap pacuṅkuḻavi vāymaṭut tuṇṇavum

viṭpulam virun tayaravum

veḷḷamutam vīcuṅ karuntiraip paintukil

virittuṭut tutti viriyum



nākattu mīccuṭikai naṭuvaṭ kiṭantamaṭa

naṅkaiyaip peṟṟu maṟṟan

nākaṇait tuñcutaṉ ṟantaikku vantutavu

naḷiṉak kuḻantai kākka



pākattu marakatak kuṉṟeṉṟor tamaṉiyak

kuṉṟoṭu kiḷaittu niṉṟa

pavaḷat taṭaṅkuṉ ṟuḷakkaṇṇa teṉṟap

parañcuṭar muṭikku muṭimūṉ



ṟākat tamaittuppi ṉorumuṭit taṉmuṭivait

taṇaṅkaracu vīṟṟi rukkum

apiṭēka valliyai yaḷikkula muḻakkukuḻa

laṅkayaṟ kaṇṇa mutaiyē. (5)



tēvēntiraṉ

vēṟu



cuḻiyuṅ karuṅkaṭ kuṇṭakaḻi

cuvaṟṟuñ cuṭarvēṟ kiritiritta

tōṉṟaṟ kaḷittuc cuṟavuyartta

cokkap perumāṉ cekkarmuṭi



poḻiyun taraṅkak kaṅkaiviraip

puṉalkāl pāyccat taḻaintuviri

puvaṉan taṉipūt taruḷpaḻutta

poṉṉaṅ koṭiyaip purakkavaḻin



tiḻiyun tuṇarkkaṟ pakattiṉaṟa

vitaḻttēṉ kuṭittuk kumaṭṭiyeti

reṭukkum ciṟaivaṇ ṭuvaṭṭuṟavuṇ

ṭiraikkak karaikku matakkaluḻik



kuḻiyuñ ciṟuka ṇēṟṟurumuk

kuralveṇ puyaluṅ karumpuyaluṅ

kuṉṟaṅ kulaiya vukaittēṟuṅ

kulicat taṭakkai puttaḷē. (6)


tirumakaḷ

vēṟu



veñcūṭṭu neṭṭuṭal virikkum paṭappāyaṉ

mīmicait tuñcu nīla

mēkatti ṉākattu viṭucuṭarp paṭalaimaṇi

meṉpara luṟutta nontu



pañcūṭṭu cīṟaṭi pataittumataṉ veṅkatirp

paṭumiḷa veyiṟku ṭaintum

paintuḻāyk kāṭuviri taṇṇiḻa lotunkumor

pacuṅkoṭiyai yañca lippā



mañcūṭ ṭakaṭṭuneṭu vāṉmukaṭu turuvumoru

maṟaiyōti mañca likka

maṟitiraic ciṟaiviriyu māyira mukakkaṭavuṇ

mantāki ṉippe yartta



ceñcūṭṭu veḷḷō timaṅkuṭi yirukkumvaḷar

ceñcaṭaik karumi ṭaṟṟut

tēvukku muṉṉiṉṟa teyvattai mummulait

tiruvaip purakka veṉṟē. (7)



kalaimakaḷ



veḷḷit takaṭṭuneṭ ṭēṭaviḻt tiṉṉicai

virumpuñ curumpar pāṭa

viḷainaṟavu kakkum polaṉ pokuṭ ṭalarkamala

vīṭṭuk koḻit teṭuttut



teḷḷit teḷikkum tamiḻkaṭali ṉaṉpiṉain

tiṇaiyeṉa veṭutta viṟainūṟ

ṟeḷḷamutu kūṭṭuṇumor veḷḷōti mattiṉiru

cīṟaṭi muṭippam vaḷarpaiṅ



kiḷḷaikku maḻalaip pacuṅkutalai yoḻukutīṅ

kiḷaviyuṅ kaḷi mayiṟkuk

kiḷariḷañ cāyalu navvikku nōkkumviri

kiñcukac cūṭṭa racaṉap



piḷḷaikku maṭanaṭaiyu muṭaṉoṭu makaḷirkkor

pētamaiyu mutavi mutirāp

piḷḷaimaiyiṉ vaḷḷaṉmai koḷḷumoru pāṇṭip

pirāṭṭiyaik kākka veṉṟē. (8)



turkkai



vaṭipaṭṭa mukkuṭumi vaṭivē ṟirittiṭṭu

vaḷaikaruṅ kōṭṭu mōṭṭu

makiṭaṅ kaviḻttuk kaṭāṅkaviḻk kuñciṟukaṇ

mālyāṉai vīṅka vāṅkun



tuṭipaṭṭa koṭinuṇ ṇucuppiṟ kuṭainteṉac

cuṭukaṭaik kaṉali tūṇṭuñ

cuḻalkaṇ muṭaṅkuḷai maṭaṅkalai yukaittēṟu

cūrarip piṇavu kākka



piṭipaṭṭa maṭanaṭaik kēkkaṟṟa kūntaṟ

piṭikkuḻāñ cuṟṟa voṟṟaip

piṟaimarup puṭaiyator kaḷiṟṟiṉaip peṟṟentai

piṭṭuṇṭu kaṭṭuṇṭu niṉ



ṟaṭipaṭṭa tirumēṉi kuḻaiyak kuḻaittiṭṭa

vaṇimaṇik kimpu rikkō

ṭākatta tākak kaṭampā ṭavikkuḷ viḷai

yāṭumor maṭappiṭi yaiyē. (9)



catta mātarkaḷ

vēṟu



kaṭakaḷi ṟutavuka pāymicaip pōrttavaḷ

kavikuvi tuṟukaliṉ vāriyait tūrttavaḷ

kaṭalvayi ṟeriyavoḷ vēliṉaip pārttavaḷ

kaṭikamaḻ tarumalar tārmuṭic cērttavaḷ



iṭiyuka vaṭalari yēṟukait tārttavaḷ

eḻutaru muḻumaṟai nūliṉiṟ kūrttavaḷ

eyiṟuko ṭuḻuteḻu pāriṉaip pērttavaḷ

eṉumiva reḻuvarka ṭāṇmuṭic cūṭṭutum



kuṭamoṭu kuṭaviyar pāṇikaik kōttiṭu

kuravaiyu malatorpa ṇāmuṭic cūṭṭaruḷ

kutikoḷa naṭamiṭu pāṭaluk kēṟpavor

kuḻalicai paḻakaḷi pāṭiṭak kēṭṭuṭai



maṭalaviḻ tuḷapana ṟāveṭut tūṟṟiṭa

maḻakaḷi ṟeṉaveḻu kārmukac cūṟpuyal

varavaru miḷaiyaku māriyaik kōṭṭeyiṉ

maturaiyil vaḷarkavu māriyaik kākkavē. (10)



muppattu mūvar

vēṟu



amaril venniṭuma vutiyar piṉṉiṭumo

rapayar muṉṉiṭuva ṉattokka vōṭavum

aḷavu memmuṭaiya tiṟaiyi teṉṉamuṭi

yaraca reṇṇilaror muṟṟattu vāṭavum



akila maṉṉaravar ticaiyiṉ maṉṉariva

ramara reṉṉumurai tikkeṭṭu mūṭavum

amuta veṇmatiyiṉ marapai yuṉṉiyuṉi

yalari yaṇṇalmuḻu veppattu mūḻkavum



kumari poṉṉivaiyai poruṇai naṉṉatikaḷ

kutikoḷ viṇṇatiyiṉ mikkuk kulāvavuṅ

kuvaṭu teṉmalaiyi ṉikara tiṉmaicurar

kuṭikoḷ poṉmalaitu tittuppa rāvavuṅ



kumarar muṉṉiruva ramara raṉṉaiyivaḷ

kumari yiṉṉamume ṉaccittar pāṭavuṅ

kuravai vimmavara makaḷir maṇṇileḻil

kulavu kaṉṉiyarkaḷ kaikkokka vāṭavum



kamalaṉ muṉṉiyiṭu maraca vaṉṉameḻu

kaṭali laṉṉamuṭa ṉaṭpukkai kūṭavuṅ

kariya cemmaloṭu miḷaiya cemmalviṭu

karuṭaṉ mañcaiyoṭor kaṭcikku ḷūṭavuṅ



kaṭavi viṇṇaracu naṭavum vemmuṉaiya

kaḷiṟu kaimmalaicel koppattu vīḻavuṅ

kaṉaka maṉṉutaṭa naḷiṉi tuṉṉiyiru

kamala miṉṉumoru paṟpattuṇ mēvavum



imaya meṉṉamaṉu muṟaiko ṭeṉṉarume

miṟaiyai naṉmaruke ṉappeṟṟu vāḻavum

evarkol paṇṇavarka ḷevarkoṉ maṇṇavarka

ḷetukol poṉṉulake ṉattaṭṭu māṟavum



eḻilcey teṉmaturai taḻaiya mummulaiyo

ṭeḻume ṉammaṉai vaṉappukkor kāvalar

iruva reṇmarpati ṉoruvar paṉṉiruva

reṉum viṇṇavarkaṇ muppattu mūvarē. (11)

2-vatu ceṅkīraip paruvam







nīrāṭṭi yāṭṭupoṟ cuṇṇan timirntaḷḷi

neṟṟiyiṟ ṟoṭṭiṭṭa veṇ

ṇīṟṟiṉoṭu puṇṭarak kīṟṟukku mēṟṟiṭavor

nittilac cuṭṭi cāttit



tārāṭṭu cūḻiyak koṇṭaiyu muṭittut

talaippaṇi tirutti muttiṉ

ṟaṇṇoḷi tatumpuṅ kutampaiyoṭu kātukkor

tamaṉiyak koppu miṭṭup



pārāṭṭu pāṇṭip peruntēvi tirumulaip

pālamuta mūṭṭi yoruniṉ

pāṉāṟu kumutaṅ kaṉintūṟu tēṟaltaṉ

paṭṭāṭai maṭina ṉaippac



cīrāṭṭi vaittumut tāṭum pacuṅkiḷḷai

ceṅkīrai yāṭi yaruḷē

teṉṉaṟku mampoṉmalai maṉṉaṟku morucelvi

ceṅkīrai yāṭi yaruḷē. (1)



uṇṇilā vuvakaip peruṅkaḷi tuḷumpaniṉ

ṟuṉṟirut tātai niṉṉai

yorumuṟai karampotti varukeṉa vaḻaittiṭumu

ṉōṭit tavaḻntu ceṉṟu



taṇṇulā maḻalaip pacuṅkutalai yamutiṉiya

tāyvayiṟu kuḷira vūṭṭit

taṭamārpa niṟaikuṅ kumaccē ṟaḷaintupoṟ

ṟāṭōy taṭakkai paṟṟip



paṇṇulā vaṭitamiḻp paintāmam viriyum

paṇaittō ḷeruttamēṟip

pācoḷiya marakatat tirumēṉi paccaip

pacuṅkatir tatumpa maṇivāyt



teṇṇilā viriya niṉṟāṭum pacuntōkai

ceṅkīrai yāṭi yaruḷē

teṉṉaṟku mampoṉmalai maṉṉaṟku morucelvi

ceṅkīrai yāṭi yaruḷē. (2)



cuṟṟuneṭu nēmic cuvarkkicaiya veṭṭuc

cuvarkkā ṉiṟutti mērut

tūṇoṉṟu naṭunaṭṭu veḷimukaṭu mūṭiyiru

cuṭar viḷakkiṭṭu muṟṟa



eṟṟupuṉa liṟkaḻuvu puvaṉap paḻaṅkala

meṭuttaṭuk kippu tukkū

ḻiṉṉamuta muñcamait taṉṉai nīpaṉmuṟai

yiḻaittiṭa vaḻitta ḻittōr



muṟṟaveḷi yiṟṟiriyu mattap perumpittaṉ

muṉṉiṉṟu tontamiṭavu

muṉiyātu vaikalu meṭuttaṭuk kipperiya

mūtaṇṭa kūṭamūṭuñ



ciṟṟilviḷai yāṭumoru pacciḷam peṇpiḷḷai

ceṅkīrai yāṭi yaruḷē

teṉṉaṟku mampoṉmalai maṉṉaṟku morucelvi

ceṅkīrai yāṭi yaruḷē. (3)



maṅkulpaṭu kantarac cuntarak kaṭavuṭku

maḻakatirk kaṟṟai cuṟṟum

vāṇayaṉa mūṉṟuṅ kuḷirntamuta kalaitalai

maṭuppak kaṭaikka ṇōkkum



poṅkumatar nōkkiṟ piṟantavā ṉantap

putuppuṇari nītta maiyaṉ

puntit taṭattiṉai nirappavaḻi yaṭiyarpāṟ

pōkacā karama ṭuppa

vaṉait tuyirka ḷuntaḷirppa

varuṇmaṭai tiṟantukaṭai veḷḷam perukkeṭut

talaiyeṟin tukaḷa vukaḷuñ
