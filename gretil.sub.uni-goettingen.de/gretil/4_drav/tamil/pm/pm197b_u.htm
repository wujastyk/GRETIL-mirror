kāppu
nēricai veṇpā

pācat taḷaiyaṟuttup pāvak kaṭalkalakki
nēcat taḷaippaṭṭu niṟkumē - mācaṟṟa
kārār varaiyīṉṟa kaṉṉip piṭiyaḷitta
ōrāṉai vante ṉuḷattu. 1


mayaṅkicaik koccakkalippā

--- taravu ---

nīrkoṇṭa kaṭalāṭai nilamakaḷuk kaṇiyāṉa
kārkoṇṭa poḻiṟkācik kaṭinakaraṅ kuḷirtūṅka
iṭamaruṅkiṟ ciṟumaruṅkuṟ peruntaṭaṅka ṇiṉṉamirtum
caṭaimaruṅki ṉeṭuntiraikkaip peṇṇamirtun talaiciṟappak
kaṇkatuvu kaṭavuṇmaṇi terintamarar kammiyaṉcey
viṇkatuvu polaṅkuṭumi vimāṉattiṉ micaippolintōy. .......(1)
niṟpaṉavun tavaḻvaṉavu naṭappaṉavu māynilattuk
kaṟpamaḷa vilakaṇṭu muṟukaḷaikaṇ kāṇāmē
paḻaṅkaṇuṟu muyirkaṭuyark kaṭaṉīttup paraṅkaruṇai
vaḻaṅkupara māṉanta mākkaṭaliṟ ṟiḷaittāṭa
uraiyāta paḻamaṟaiyiṉ mutaleḻutti ṉoṇporuḷai
varaiyātu koṭuttiṭuniṉ vaḷḷaṉmai vāḻttutumē. .......(2)

--- tāḻicai ---

nīreḻuttuk kottavuṭa ṉīttārkku nīnavilva
tōreḻuttē muḻutumava revvaṇṇa muṇarvatuvē. .......(1)

eṉpaṇiva tuṭuppatutō lempirāṉ ṟamarkaḷavar
muṉpaṇiyum pēṟuṭaiyār ticaimukaṉu mukuntaṉumē. .......(2)

ceṭikoṇmuṭaip puḻukkūṭē ciṟṟaṭiyō miṭutiṟaimaṟ
ṟaṭikaḷaṭi yārkkaḷippa tāṉantap peruvāḻvē. .......(3)

paṟpakaṉōṟ ṟaruntavarum peṟaṟkariya parantāmam
eṟpuṭalviṟ ṟaḷiyēmuṅ koḷappeṟuva tiṟumpūtē. .......(4)

niṇampuṇarveṇ ṭalaikkalaṉko ṉēriḻaimut tittiruvai
maṇampuṇarvārk kaiyaṉaruṇ maṇavāḷak kōlamē. .......(5)

muṭaittalaiyiṟ palikoḷvāṉ mūvulaku mavaravartaṅ
kaṭaittalaiyiṟ ṟirivatukol yāmpeṟuniṉ kāṇiyē. .......(6)

--- arākam ---

uḷateṉa vilateṉa voruvaro raḷavaiyiṉ
aḷaviṉi laḷaviṭa lariyato ruruviṉai. .......(1)

ituveṉa larumaiyi ṉeḻutaru moḻikaḷum
atuvala veṉumeṉi ṉevaruṉai yaṟipavar. .......(2)

avaṉava ḷatuveṉu mavaikaḷi ṉuḷaṉalaṉ
evaṉava ṉivaṉeṉa vetirtaru takaimaiyai. .......(3)

aṟipava raṟiviṉu ḷaṟivuko ṭaṟivuṟu
neṟiyala toruvaru maṟivaru nilaimaiyai. .......(4)

--- nāṟcīrōraṭi ampōtaraṅkam ---

āṇoṭu peṇṇuru vamaittu niṉṟaṉai.
pūṇmulai kalantumaim pulaṉum veṉṟaṉai.
eṇvakai yuṟuppiṉō ruruve ṭuttaṉai.
toṉmaṟaip paṉuvaliṉ ṟoṭaito ṭuttaṉai.

-- muccīrōraṭi ampōtaraṅkam --

vaṭavarai kuḻaiya vaḷaittaṉai.
malaimakaṇ mulaika ṭiḷaittaṉai.
viṭamamir tamara viḷaittaṉai.
vicayaṉo ṭamarcey tiḷaittaṉai.
varicilai vataṉai yerittaṉai.
matakari yurivai tarittaṉai.
arumaṟai teriya virittaṉai.
alakilpal kalaika ṭerittaṉai.

-- irucīrōraṭi ampōtaraṅkam --



aḻalvi ḻittaṉaipavamo ḻittaṉai.
āṟa ṇintaṉai māṟa ṇintaṉai..
maḻuva lattiṉai muḻuna lattiṉai.
māna ṭattiṉai māṉi ṭattiṉai..
alaki ṟantaṉai talaici ṟantaṉai..
aruḷcu rantaṉai iruṭu rantaṉai..
ulaka ḷittaṉai tamiḻte ḷittaṉai.
oṉṟu māyiṉai palavu māyiṉai..


-- tāḻicai --

alakilpala puvaṉaṅka ḷaṭaṅkalumuṇ ṭoḻippāykkuk
kolaiviṭamuṇ ṭaṉaiyeṉṟu kūṟuvatōr vīṟāmē. .......(1)

payiṉmūṉṟu puvaṉamuṅkaṭ poṟikkiraiyāp pālippāyk
keyiṉmūṉṟu merimaṭuttā yeṉpatumō ricaiyāmē. .......(2)

aṭiyavarē mukkuṟumpu maṟaveṟintā reṉiṉaṭikaḷ
viṭukaṇaiviṟ kāmaṉainī veṉṟatumōr viyappāmē. .......(3)

ikkūṟṟiṉ ṟirunāmat torukūṟṟuk kilakkeṉṟāl
akkūṟṟaṅ kumaittaṉaiyeṉ ṟicaippatumō raṟputamē. .......(1)

eṉavāṅku

-- curitakam --

ulakucūṟ koṇṭa talaiviyu nīyum
malaipaka veṟinta maḻaviḷaṅ kuḻaviyai
amutamūṟ ṟirukkuṅ kumutavāyt tēṟal
vaṇṭuki ṉaṉaippa maṭittalat tiruttik
kaṇkaḷiṟ parukiyak kāmaru kuḻavi
eḻutāk kiḷavi yiṉcuvai paḻutta
maḻalainā ṟamirtam vāymaṭut tuṇṇac
ceñcevi niṟaittanum mañcevik kaṭikaḷeṉ
puṉmoḻik kaṭukkoḷap pukaṭṭiṉaṉ
iṉṉaruḷ viḻaikuvā yiṟumpū tuṭaittē. 2


nēricai veṇpā

uṭaiyā ḷakilēcark kōṅkumulaik kōṭṭiṉ
aṭaiyāḷa miṭṭuvaiyā ḷāṉāṟ - kaṭaiyilavar
cevvaṇṇam peṟṟār tiraḷoṭuniṟ kiṉṟārai
evvaṇṇaṅ kaṇṭiṟaiñcu vēm. 3


tūtu
kaṭṭaḷaik kalittuṟai

iṟaivaḷaik kākam pakuntaḷit tāraki lēcarkoṉṟai
naṟaivaḷaik kummuṭi yāraṭik kēkaṅkai naṉṉatiyiṉ
tuṟaivaḷaik kuṅkuru kīruru kīreṉṟu tūmoḻikaik
kuṟaivaḷaik kummuṅkaḷ pēriṭṭa tāṟceṉṟu kūṟiṭumē. 4


puyavakuppu
canta viruttam

iṭamaṟa miṭaitaru kaṭavuḷar maṭaviyar
eṟitaru kavarini ḻaṟkaṭ ṭuyiṉṟaṉa
iṉavaḷai koṭumata ṉiṭucaya viruteṉa
iṟaiyava ḷeḻutucu vaṭṭuk kicaintaṉa
iruvaru nikareṉa varicilai vicayaṉo
ṭetirporu camarilai ḷaippuṟ ṟiruntaṉa
iṇaiyaṭi paraviya malaṭimu ṉutaviya
iṭiyali ṉuṇavoru koṭṭaip parintaṉa
paṭavara vumiḻtaru maṇiveyil viṭavaḷar
parutiyo ṭeḻumuta yattiṟ polintaṉa
parukumi ṉamirteṉa vurukiru kaviñrkaḷ
paṉuvaliṉ maturavi caikkuk kuḻaintaṉa
paṭaroḷi viṭucuṭar valayama teṉavoru
paruvarai neṭuvile ṭuttuc cumantaṉa
pararpura meriyoṭu pukaiyeḻa malarmakaḷ
paṇaimulai taḻuvuca rattait turantaṉa
maṭalaviḻ taṭamala ritaḻiyi ṉiḻitaru
matumaḻai yaruviku ḷittuk kiḷarntaṉa
vaḻitara vutiramu niṇamoṭu kuṭarkaḷum
varunara kariyiṉma tattait taṭintaṉa
matakari yuriyataḷ kulakiri mutukiṉiṉ
maḻaimuki ṟavaḻvate ṉappoṟ pamaintaṉa
malipukaḻ nilavoṭu maṭutiṟal veyileḻa
matikatir valamvaru veṟpot tuniṉṟaṉa
kuṭavaḷai tuṟaitoṟu muṭunirai yeṉavari
kuḷirnila veḻavumiḻ muttait taṭaṅkarai
kulaviya paṭarciṟai maṭavaṉa moṭucila
kurukukaḷ ciṉaiyoṭa ṇaittut tuyiṉṟiṭu
kuraipuṉal varanati curartaru murukaviḻ
koḻumalar citaṟavi muttat tuviṇṭoṭu
kulakiri yutaviya vaḷariḷa vaṉamulai
koḻunarta maḻakiya koṟṟap puyaṅkaḷē. 5

nālvarkku mammaṟaip poruḷkūṟa

vaṉattumem mitayattu muruntōṉē.8

taṇṇoṉṟu naṟaiyitaḻit tāreṉṟā

ḷitukaṇṭā leḻuttoṉ ṟōtat
tuṇṇeṉṟu varuvareṉat tuṇintaṉaḷō
vaṟiyēṉit tōkai tāṉē. 10

taṇṇu lāmpoḻiṟ kācit teruviṉīr
taritti ṭuntavak kōlamuñ cūlamum
peṇṇo ṭāṭumap piccaṉuk kottalāṟ
picci yāreṉum pērtarit tāṭuvīr
veṇṇi lāmukiḻk kuṅkuṟu mūralāl
vīṇi lēyem puratteri yiṭṭanīr
kaṇṇi ṉālumik kāmaṉaik kāyntiṭiṟ
kaṭavu ṇīreṉ ṟiṟaiñcutuṅ kāṇumē. 16

kāṇuṅ kāṇu natikaḷel lāmpuṉaṟ
kaṅkai yēyaṅ kuḷateyvam yāvaiyum
tāṇu veṅka ḷakilēca rēmaṟṟait
talaṅkaḷ yāvun taṭamatiṟ kāciyē

pēṇu māṟu peṟavēṇṭu mappuṟam
pēyo ṭāṭiṉu māṭap peṟutumē. 17

ceṟṟu mīḷap paṭaikkavum vēṇṭumē
