1. tirumāl
aru maṟaip poruḷ

āyiram viritta aṇaṅkuṭai arun talai
tī umiḻ tiṟaloṭu muṭimicai aṇavara,
māyuṭai malar mārpiṉ, mai il vāl vaḷai mēṉic
cēy uyar paṇaimicai eḻil vēḻam ēntiya,
vāy vāṅkum vaḷai nāñcil, oru kuḻai oruvaṉai; 5

erimalar ciṉaiiya kaṇṇai; pūvai
virimalar puraiyum mēṉiyai; mēṉit
tiru ñemirntu amarnta mārpiṉai; mārpil
terimaṇi piṟaṅkum pūṇiṉai; māl varai
eri tirintaṉṉa poṉ puṉai uṭukkaiyai- 10

cēval am koṭiyōy! niṉ vala vayiṉ niṟuttum
ēval uḻantamai kūṟum,
nā val antaṇar aru maṟaip poruḷē.

amar veṉṟa kaṇai

iṇaipiri aṇi tuṇi paṇi eri purai
viṭar iṭu cuṭar paṭar polam puṉai viṉaimalar 15

neri tiraṭeri purai taṉa miku taṉa muraṇ miku
kaṭaṟaru maṇiyoṭum muttu yātta nēraṇi
neṟi ceṟi veṟi uṟu muṟal viṟal vaṇaṅku aṇaṅku vil
tār aṇi tuṇi maṇi veyil uṟaḻ eḻil pukaḻ alar mārpiṉ
eri vayira nuti eṟi paṭai eruttu malai ivar navaiyiṉiṟ 20

ṟuṇi paṭal iṉa maṇi veyil uṟaḻ eḻil nakku
imai iruḷ akala muṟu kiṟuku puri oru puri nāḷmalar
malar ilakiṉa vaḷar paritiyiṉ oḷi maṇi mārpu aṇi
maṇam mika nāṟu uruviṉa virai vaḷi miku kaṭu vicai
uṭuvuṟu talai nirai itaḻ aṇi vayiṟiriya amararaip 25

pōreḻuntu uṭaṉṟu iraittu uraiiya tāṉavar
ciram umiḻ puṉal poḻipu iḻintu uram
utirpu atirpu alam toṭā amar veṉṟa kaṇai.

collil aṭaṅkāp perum pukaḻ
'poruvēm' eṉṟavar matam tapak kaṭantu,
ceru mēmpaṭṭa ceyir tīr aṇṇal! 30

iruvar tātai! ilaṅku pūṇ māal!
teruḷa niṉ varavu aṟital
maruḷ aṟu tērcci muṉaivarkkum aritē:
aṉṉa marapiṉ aṉaiyōy! niṉṉai
iṉṉaṉ eṉṟu uraittal emakku evaṉ eḷitu? 35

arumai naṟku aṟiyiṉum, ārvam niṉvayiṉ
perumaiyiṉ vallā yām ivaṇ moḻipavai
melliya eṉāa veṟāatu, alli am
tiru maṟu mārpa! nī aruḷal vēṇṭum.

tuti moḻikaḷ

viṟal miku viḻuc cīr antaṇar kākkum 40

aṟaṉum, ārvalarkku aḷiyum, nī;
tiṟaṉ ilōrt tiruttiya tītu tīr ciṟappiṉ
maṟaṉum, māṟṟalarkku aṇaṅkum, nī;
am kaṇ ēr vāṉattu aṇi nilāt tikaḻtarum
tiṅkaḷum, teṟu katirk kaṉaliyum, nī; 45

ain talai uyiriya aṇaṅkuṭai arun tiṟal
maintuṭai oruvaṉum, maṭaṅkalum, nī;
nalam muḻutu aḷaiiya pukar aṟu kāṭcip
pulamum, pūvaṉum, nāṟṟamum, nī;
valaṉ uyar eḻiliyum, māka vicumpum, 50

nilaṉum, nīṭiya imayamum, nī.
ataṉāl,
'iṉṉōr aṉaiyai; iṉaiyaiyāl' eṉa,
aṉṉōr yām ivaṇ kāṇāmaiyiṉ,
poṉ aṇi nēmi valam koṇṭu ēntiya 55

maṉṉuyir mutalvaṉai ātaliṉ,
niṉṉōr aṉaiyai, niṉ pukaḻōṭum polintē!
niṉ okkum pukaḻ niḻalavai;
poṉ okkum uṭaiyavai;
puḷḷiṉ koṭiyavai; puri vaḷaiyiṉavai; 60

eḷḷunark kaṭantu aṭṭa ikal nēmiyavai;
maṇṇuṟu maṇi pāy uruviṉavai;
eṇ iṟanta pukaḻavai; eḻil mārpiṉavai,
āṅku,
kāmaru cuṟṟamoṭu oruṅku niṉ aṭiyuṟai
yām iyaintu oṉṟupu vaikalum polika! eṉa,
ēmuṟu neñcattēm paravutum-
vāymoḻip pulava! niṉ tāḷ-niḻal toḻutē; 68

ōṅku uyar vāṉiṉ vāṅku vil puraiyum
pūṇ aṇi kavaiiya ār aṇi nittila
nittila matāṇi attaku mati maṟuc 30

ceyyōḷ cērnta niṉ mācu il akalam-
vaḷar tirai maṇṇiya kiḷar poṟi nāppaṇ
vai vāl maruppiṉ kaḷiṟu maṇaṉ ayarpu
'puḷḷi nilaṉum puraipaṭal aritu' eṉa
uḷḷunar uraippōr uraiyoṭu ciṟantaṉṟu. 35

paṭaic ciṟappu

oṭiyā uḷḷamoṭu uruttu, oruṅku uṭaṉ iyaintu,
iṭi etir kaḻaṟum-kāl uṟaḻpu eḻuntavar
koṭi aṟupu iṟupu, cevi ceviṭu paṭupu,
muṭikaḷ atira, paṭinilai taḷara,
naṉi mural vaḷai muṭi aḻipu, iḻipu, 40

talai iṟupu tāroṭu puraḷa-
nilai tolaipu, vēr, tūr, maṭal,
kuruku, paṟiyā nīḷ irum paṉaimicaip
pala patiṉāyiram kulai tarai utirvapōl-
nillātu, orumuṟai koypu kūṭi, 45

oruṅku uruṇṭu, piḷantu, nerintu, uruḷpu citaṟupu,
aḷaṟu coripu, nilam cōra,
cērār iṉ uyir cekukkum-
pōr aṭu kuricil! nī ēntiya paṭaiyē:
oṉṉār uṭaṅku uṇṇum kūṟṟam uṭalē; 50

poṉ ērpu avir aḻal nuṭakku ataṉ niṟaṉē.

tirumāl tirumēṉiyiṉ oḷi mutaliya ciṟappukkaḷ

niṉṉatu tikaḻ oḷi ciṟappu iruḷ tirumaṇi;
kaṇṇē, pukaḻcāl tāmarai alar iṇaip piṇaiyal;
vāymai, vayaṅkiya vaikal; ciṟanta
nōṉmai nāṭiṉ, iru nilam; yāvarkkum, 55

cāyal niṉatu, vāṉ niṟai-eṉṉum
nā val antaṇar aru maṟaip poruḷē:
avvum piṟavum ottaṉai; uvvum
ev vayiṉōyum nīyē.

uruvamum, uṇavum, veḷippāṭum
cevvāy uvaṇattu uyar koṭiyōyē! 60

kēḷviyuḷ kiḷanta ācāṉ uraiyum,
paṭi nilai vēḷviyuḷ paṟṟi āṭu koḷalum,
pukaḻ iyaintu icai maṟai uṟu kaṉal muṟai mūṭṭit
tikaḻ oḷi oṇ cuṭar vaḷappāṭu koḷalum,
niṉ urupuṭaṉ uṇṭi; 65

piṟar uṭampaṭuvārā
niṉṉoṭu puraiya
antaṇar kāṇum varavu.

pal pukaḻum paravalum

vāyaṭai amirtam niṉ maṉattakattu aṭaittara,
mūvā marapum ōvā nōṉmaiyum 70

cāvā marapiṉ amararkkāc ceṉṟa niṉ
... ... ... marapiṉōy niṉ aṭi
talai uṟa vaṇaṅkiṉēm, pal māṇ yāmum;
