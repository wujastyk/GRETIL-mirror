varaṃ bhikṣoḥ śīlavipattir na punar dṛṣṭivipattir iti / atas tatra tāvat kṣaṇikān nirātmakān sarvadharmān vyavalokya vyapagatasakalavikalpaḥ kṛpāśayo 'ho vatāmī satvāḥ kleśakarmādibhir upadrutāḥ /
